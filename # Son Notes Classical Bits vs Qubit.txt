# Son Notes: Classical Bits vs Qubits

## Classical Bit

A classical bit is either 0 or 1.

## Qubit

A qubit can be written as:

|ψ⟩ = a|0⟩ + b|1⟩

## What I Learned

A qubit uses amplitudes before measurement.

When measured, it becomes either 0 or 1.

## Connection to Our Project

Our Bell-state circuit uses qubits instead of classical bits.
