\# Superposition



Superposition is one of the most important ideas in quantum computing.



A regular computer bit can only be:



0



or



1



A qubit can be in a combination of both states before it is measured.



This is called superposition.



\## Example



A qubit can have:



50% chance of being 0



50% chance of being 1



until it is measured.



\## How Superposition Is Made



A Hadamard gate can create superposition.



The Hadamard gate changes a qubit from a single state into multiple possibilities.



\## Vocabulary



Superposition = multiple possibilities before measurement



Hadamard Gate = quantum gate that creates superposition



Qubit = basic unit of quantum information



Measurement = observing a quantum state
