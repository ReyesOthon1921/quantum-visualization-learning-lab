\# Bell State Notes



\## What is a Bell State?



A Bell state is a special quantum state involving two qubits.



The qubits become connected through entanglement.



\## Circuit Steps



1\. Hadamard gate creates superposition

2\. CNOT gate creates entanglement

3\. Measurement checks outcomes



\## Expected Results



Most common outcomes:



\- 00

\- 11



\## New Vocabulary



\### Qubit

A quantum version of a classical bit.



\### Superposition

A qubit can exist in multiple states at once.



\### Entanglement

Two qubits become correlated together.



\### Measurement

Reading a qubit collapses it into a classical value.
