\# Quantum Vocabulary



\## Qubit

A quantum information unit.



\## Superposition

A qubit existing in multiple possible states.



\## Entanglement

Two qubits connected together.



\## Quantum Circuit

A sequence of quantum operations.



\## Hadamard Gate

Creates superposition.



\## CNOT Gate

Creates entanglement.



\## Measurement

Converts quantum information into classical information.



\## Histogram

A graph showing measurement outcomes.
