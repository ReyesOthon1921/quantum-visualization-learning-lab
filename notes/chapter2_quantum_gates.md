\# Quantum Gates



\## Classical Gates



Classical computers use logic gates.



Examples:



\- AND

\- OR

\- XOR

\- NAND



\## Quantum Gates



Quantum computers use unitary operators.



Quantum gates change amplitudes.



\## Important Gates



\### Hadamard Gate



Creates superposition.



\### CNOT Gate



Creates entanglement.



\### Pauli X



Acts like a bit flip.



\### Pauli Z



Changes phase.



\### Pauli Y



Combination of X and Z.



\## Observation



Quantum gates are reversible.



Classical gates often are not reversible.
