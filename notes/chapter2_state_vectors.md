\# State Vectors and Amplitudes



\## Single Qubit



A quantum state can be written as:



|ψ⟩ = a|0⟩ + b|1⟩



The values a and b are called amplitudes.



\## Normalization



All probabilities must add to 1.



|a|² + |b|² = 1



\## Measurement



Probability of measuring:



|0⟩ = |a|²



|1⟩ = |b|²



\## Observation



Quantum states are represented by vectors.



The amplitudes determine the likelihood of each measurement outcome.
